SARS eFiling Guide

This guide will walk you through the process of submitting your tax return via SARS eFiling.

Step 1: Register for eFiling

If you haven't already, register for SARS eFiling on the official SARS website. You will need your ID number and personal details.

Step 2: Log In and Request ITR12

Log in to your eFiling profile. Navigate to 'Returns' -> 'File a Return' -> 'Income Tax (ITR12)' and request your ITR12 for the relevant tax year.

Step 3: Complete Your ITR12

Fill in all relevant sections of your ITR12 using the information from your IRP5 and any other income/deduction certificates. Our Professional Report can assist you with this.

Step 4: Submit Your Return

Review your completed ITR12 carefully. Once satisfied, submit your return. SARS will then issue an assessment (ITA34).

Step 5: Payment or Refund

Based on your ITA34, you will either need to make a payment to SARS or you will receive a refund. Ensure you adhere to payment deadlines.
