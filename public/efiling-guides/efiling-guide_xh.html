Isikhokelo Sakho Esilula se-SARS eFiling

I-SARS eFiling yiqonga le-elekthronikhi lokungenisa iingxelo zakho zerhafu kwi-intanethi. Ikhuselekile, ilula, kwaye ikhawuleza kunokuthumela ngesandla. Landela esi sikhokelo senyathelo nganye ukugqiba ingxelo yakho yerhafu ka-2025.

Inyathelo 1: Bhalisa kwi-eFiling

Abasebenzisi bokuqala:

  1. Tyelela www.sars.gov.za uze ucofe "eFiling"
  2. Cofa "Bhalisa" uze ukhethe "Umntu"
  3. Faka inombolo yakho ye-ID, inombolo yereferensi yerhafu yengeniso, kunye neenkcukacha zonxibelelwano
  4. Yenza igama lomsebenzisi nephasiwedi
  5. I-SARS iya kukuthumelela i-PIN nge-SMS okanye nge-imeyili
  6. Sebenzisa i-PIN ukuvula iprofayili yakho ye-eFiling

Into oya kuyifuna:

  • Inombolo ye-ID yaseMzantsi Afrika okanye ipaspoti
  • Inombolo yereferensi yerhafu yengeniso (ukuba unayo)
  • Idilesi ye-imeyili esebenzayo
  • Inombolo yefowuni yaseMzantsi Afrika
  • Iinkcukacha zebhanki

Inyathelo 2: Qokelela Amaxwebhu Akho

Amaxwebhu abalulekileyo onyaka werhafu ka-2025:

  • Isatifikethi se-IRP5: Esivela kumqeshi wakho (sibonisa umvuzo, irhafu ekhutshiweyo, njl.)
  • Izatifikethi ze-IT3(a): Zenzala efunyenwe kwiibhanki
  • Izatifikethi ze-IT3(b): Zamadividendi afunyenweyo
  • Isatifikethi Soncedo Lwempilo: Sibonisa amagalelo kunye nezibonelelo
  • Isatifikethi Sengxowa-mali Yomhlala-phantsi: Sibonisa amagalelo epenshini/engxowa-mali yolondolozo
  • Isatifikethi Sengxowa-mali ye-RA: Samagalelo e-annuity yomhlala-phantsi
  • Iirekhodi Zengeniso Yokuqesha: Iingxelo zengeniso neendleko
  • Iingxelo Zotyalo-mali: Inzuzo/ilahleko yemali, utyalo-mali lwamazwe angaphandle
  • Iirekhodi Zeshishini: Ukuba uzisebenzela okanye unengeniso yeshishini
  • Iirisithi Zezipho: Iirisithi zeCandelo 18A zezipho ezisuswa kwirhafu

Inyathelo 3: Ngena uze Uqale Ingxelo Yakho

  1. Yiya ku-www.sars.gov.za uze ucofe "eFiling"
  2. Faka igama lakho lomsebenzisi nephasiwedi
  3. Cofa "Iingxelo" kwimenyu ephambili
  4. Khetha "Ingxelo Yerhafu Yengeniso (ITR12)" yabantu
  5. Khetha unyaka werhafu ka-2025
  6. Cofa "Yenza Ingxelo" ukuqala
Icebiso: I-SARS inokugcwalisa kwangaphambili enye inkcazelo evela kumaqela angaphandle (abaqeshi, iibhanki, njl.). Soloko uqinisekisa ukuba le nkcazelo ichanekile kwaye iphelele.

Inyathelo 4: Gqibezela Icandelo Ngalinye

Amacandelo aphambili okuwagqibezela:

Inkcazelo Yomntu
  • Qinisekisa iinkcukacha zakho zomntu
  • Hlaziya inkcazelo yonxibelelwano xa kufuneka
  • Qinisekisa iinkcukacha zebhanki zokubuyiselwa
Ingeniso (Imali Engenayo)
  • Ingeniso yomsebenzi evela kwi-IRP5
  • Inzala namadividendi
  • Ingeniso yokuqesha
  • Ingeniso yeshishini
  • Ingeniso yamazwe angaphandle
Ukususa
  • Amagalelo engxowa-mali yomhlala-phantsi
  • Amagalelo oncedo lwempilo
  • Izipho kwii-PBOs
  • Iindleko zeshishini
  • Iindleko zeofisi yasekhaya
Amatyala Erhafu
  • Ityala lerhafu lemirhumo yeskimu sempilo
  • Iindleko ezongezelelweyo zempilo
  • Amatyala erhafu yamazwe angaphandle

Inyathelo 5: Phonononga uze Ungenise

  1. Phonononga onke amacandelo: Khangela ukufakwa ngakunye ukuqinisekisa ukuchaneka
  2. Sebenzisa ikhalkhuletha: I-eFiling iya kubala irhafu yakho ngokuzenzekelayo
  3. Khangela isishwankathelo: Phonononga uxanduva lwakho lwerhafu olupheleleyo okanye ukubuyiselwa
  4. Qinisekisa ingxelo: I-eFiling iya kukhangela iimpazamo
  5. Ngenisa ingxelo yakho: Cofa "Ngenisa" xa wonelisekile
  6. Printa/gcina ukuqinisekisa: Gcina ukuvuma kokufumana
Okubalulekileyo: Nje ukuba ungenisile, awukwazi ukwenza utshintsho lula. Unokufuna ukucela ukuphononongwa okanye ufake isikhalazo ngolungelelwaniso olubalulekileyo.

Inyathelo 6: Emva Kokungenisa

Okwenzeka okulandelayo:

  • Uvavanyo: I-SARS iya kucubungula ingxelo yakho (ngokuqhelekileyo kwiintsuku ezingama-21 zoshishino)
  • Isaziso: Uya kufumana i-SMS/imeyili xa uvavanyo lwakho lukulungele
  • Khangela uvavanyo lwakho: Ngena kwi-eFiling ukubona Isaziso Sovavanyo lwakho
  • Ukubuyiselwa: Ukuba kufuneka ubuyiselwe, kuya kuhlawulwa kwi-akhawunti yakho yebhanki ebhalisiweyo
  • Iintlawulo: Ukuba unesikweletu serhafu, hlawula ngomhla omisiweyo ukuphepha izohlwayo nenzala

Iinketho zentlawulo ukuba unesikweletu serhafu:

  • Intlawulo ye-eFiling kwi-intanethi
  • Ubhankiso lwe-intanethi
  • Iintlawulo ze-ATM
  • Amasebe eebhanki
  • Amasebe e-SARS

Amacebiso Abalulekileyo Empumelelweni

Gcina Iirekhodi

Gcina onke amaxwebhu axhasayo iminyaka emi-5. I-SARS inokuwacela ngexesha lophando okanye ukuphononongwa.

Faka Kwangoko

Ngenisa ingxelo yakho kude kakhulu phambi komhla wokugqibela ukuphepha izohlwayo kwaye ufumane ukubuyiselwa ngokukhawuleza.

Khangela Kabini

Qinisekisa onke amanani ngokuchasene nezatifikethi neengxelo zakho. Iimpazamo zinokubangela uphando okanye izohlwayo.

Gcina Inkqubela

I-eFiling igcina umsebenzi wakho ngokuzenzekelayo, kodwa gcina rhoqo ngesandla ukuphepha ukulahleka kwedatha.

Fumana Uncedo

Sebenzisa izixhobo zoncedo ze-SARS, iingcali zerhafu, okanye iZiko loNxibelelwano (0800 00 7277) ukuba ufuna uncedo.

Hlaziya Iinkcukacha

Gcina iinkcukacha zakho zonxibelelwano nezebhanki zihlaziyiwe ukuqinisekisa unxibelelwano olugudileyo neentlawulo zokubuyiselwa.

Iimpazamo Eziqhelekileyo Ekufuneka Uziphepe

  • Iinkcukacha zebhanki ezingalunganga: Khangela kabini iinombolo ze-akhawunti zeentlawulo zokubuyiselwa
  • Ingeniso elahlekileyo: Bandakanya YONKE imithombo yengeniso, namanani amancinci
  • Ukubanga ukususa okungavumelekanga: Banga kuphela iindleko ezisemthethweni, ezixhasiweyo
  • Ukungahoyi idatha egcwaliswe kwangaphambili: Qinisekisa kwaye ulungise naluphi na ulwazi olungalunganga olugcwaliswe kwangaphambili
  • Ukungenisa emva kwexesha: Faka phambi komhla wokugqibela ukuphepha izohlwayo
  • Ukungagcini iirekhodi: Gcina amaxwebhu axhasa zonke iibango
