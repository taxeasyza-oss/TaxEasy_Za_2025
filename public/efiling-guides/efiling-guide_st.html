Tataiso ea Hao e Bonolo ea SARS eFiling

SARS eFiling ke sethala sa elektroniki sa ho fana ka litlaleho tsa hao tsa lekhetho inthaneteng. Se bolokehile, se bonolo, 'me se potlakile ho feta ho fana ka matsoho. Latela tataiso ena ea mohato ka mohato ho qetela tlaleho ea hao ea lekhetho ea 2025.

Mohato 1: Ngolisa bakeng sa eFiling

Basebelisi ba lekhetlo la pele:

  1. Etela www.sars.gov.za 'me o tobetse "eFiling"
  2. Tobetsa "Ngolisa" 'me o khethe "Motho ka mong"
  3. Kenya nomoro ea hao ea ID, nomoro ea reference ea lekhetho la chelete e kenang, le lintlha tsa puisano
  4. Theha lebitso la mosebelisi le password
  5. SARS e tla o romella PIN ka SMS kapa email
  6. Sebelisa PIN ho kenya tshebetsong profaele ea hao ea eFiling

Seo o tla se hlokang:

  • Nomoro ea ID ea Afrika Boroa kapa passport
  • Nomoro ea reference ea lekhetho la chelete e kenang (haeba o na le eona)
  • Aterese ea email e sebetsang
  • Nomoro ea mohala oa selula oa Afrika Boroa
  • Lintlha tsa banka

Mohato 2: Bokella Litokomane tsa Hao

Litokomane tse bohlokoa bakeng sa selemo sa lekhetho sa 2025:

  • Setifikeiti sa IRP5: Ho tloha ho mohiri oa hao (se bontsha moputso, lekhetho le ntshitsoeng, joalo-joalo)
  • Litifikeiti tsa IT3(a): Bakeng sa rente e fumaneng ho tsoa libankeng
  • Litifikeiti tsa IT3(b): Bakeng sa li-dividend tse amohetsoeng
  • Setifikeiti sa Thuso ea Bophelo bo Botle: Se bontsha litefiso le melemo
  • Setifikeiti sa Letlotlo la ho Phomola: Se bontsha litefiso tsa letlotlo la penshene/tlhokomelo
  • Setifikeiti sa Letlotlo la RA: Bakeng sa litefiso tsa annuity ea ho phomola
  • Lirekoto tsa Chelete ea Hiri: Lipolelo tsa chelete e kenang le litjeho
  • Lipolelo tsa Matsete: Phaello/tahlehelo ea capital, matsete a linaheng tse ling
  • Lirekoto tsa Khoebo: Haeba o itšebeletsa kapa o na le chelete e kenang ea khoebo
  • Li-receipt tsa Lineo: Li-receipt tsa Karolo 18A bakeng sa lineo tse ntšoang lekhetho

Mohato 3: Kena 'me o Qale Tlaleho ea Hao

  1. Ea ho www.sars.gov.za 'me o tobetse "eFiling"
  2. Kenya lebitso la hao la mosebelisi le password
  3. Tobetsa "Litlaleho" lenaneong le leholo
  4. Khetha "Tlaleho ea Lekhetho la Chelete e Kenang (ITR12)" bakeng sa batho
  5. Khetha selemo sa lekhetho sa 2025
  6. Tobetsa "Theha Tlaleho" ho qala
Keletso: SARS e ka tlatsa esale pele tlhahisoleseling e 'ngoe ho tsoa ho mekhatlo ea boraro (bahiri, libanka, joalo-joalo). Kamehla netefatsa hore tlhahisoleseling ena e nepahetseng 'me e phethahetseng.

Mohato 4: Qetela Karolo ka 'ngoe

Likarolo tse kholo tse lokelang ho qetelloa:

Tlhahisoleseling ea Motho
  • Netefatsa lintlha tsa hao tsa motho
  • Ntlafatsa tlhahisoleseling ea puisano haeba ho hlokahala
  • Netefatsa lintlha tsa banka bakeng sa ho khutlisa
Chelete e Kenang (Chelete)
  • Chelete e kenang ea mosebetsi ho tsoa ho IRP5
  • Rente le li-dividend
  • Chelete e kenang ea hiri
  • Chelete e kenang ea khoebo
  • Chelete e kenang ea linaheng tse ling
Ho Ntsha
  • Litefiso tsa letlotlo la ho phomola
  • Litefiso tsa thuso ea bophelo bo botle
  • Lineo ho PBOs
  • Litjeho tsa khoebo
  • Litjeho tsa ofisi ea lapeng
Likrediti tsa Lekhetho
  • Krediti ea lekhetho ea litefiso tsa moralo oa bophelo bo botle
  • Litjeho tse eketsehileng tsa bophelo bo botle
  • Likrediti tsa lekhetho la linaheng tse ling

Mohato 5: Hlahloba 'me o Romele

  1. Hlahloba likarolo tsohle: Hlahloba kenyelletso e 'ngoe le e 'ngoe bakeng sa ho nepahala
  2. Sebelisa calculator: eFiling e tla bala lekhetho la hao ka boithaopo
  3. Hlahloba kakaretso: Hlahloba kakaretso ea hao ea boikarabelo ba lekhetho kapa ho khutlisa
  4. Netefatsa tlaleho: eFiling e tla hlahloba liphoso
  5. Romela tlaleho ea hao: Tobetsa "Romela" ha o khotsofetse
  6. Hatisa/boloka netefatso: Boloka tumello ea ho amohela
Bohlokoa: Hang ha o romelloe, ha o khone ho etsa liphetoho habonolo. O ka 'na oa hloka ho kopa ponelopele kapa ho fana ka khangollo bakeng sa liphetoho tse kholo.

Mohato 6: Ka mor'a ho Romela

Seo se etsahalang ka mor'a moo:

  • Tekolo: SARS e tla sebetsana le tlaleho ea hao (hangata ka har'a matsatsi a 21 a khoebo)
  • Tsebiso: O tla fumana SMS/email ha tekolo ea hao e se e loketse
  • Hlahloba tekolo ea hao: Kena ho eFiling ho bona Tsebiso ea Tekolo ea hao
  • Ho khutlisa: Haeba o lokela ho khutlisoa, ho tla lefshoa akhaonteng ea hao ea banka e ngolisitsoeng
  • Litefo: Haeba o kolota lekhetho, lefa ka letsatsi le behiloeng ho qoba litlolo le rente

Likhetho tsa tefo haeba o kolota lekhetho:

  • Tefo ea eFiling inthaneteng
  • Ho banka inthaneteng
  • Litefo tsa ATM
  • Makala a libanka
  • Makala a SARS

Malebela a Bohlokoa a Katleho

Boloka Lirekoto

Boloka litokomane tsohle tse tšehetsang lilemo tse 5. SARS e ka li kopa nakong ea liteko kapa liponelopele.

Fana Pele

Romela tlaleho ea hao haholo pele ho nako ea ho fela ho qoba litlolo le ho fumana ho khutlisa kapele.

Hlahloba Habeli

Netefatsa linomoro tsohle khahlanong le litifikeiti le lipolelo tsa hao. Liphoso li ka baka liteko kapa litlolo.

Boloka Tsoelo-pele

eFiling e boloka mosebetsi oa hao ka boithaopo, empa boloka khafetsa ka letsoho ho qoba ho lahleheloa ke data.

Fumana Thuso

Sebelisa lisebelisoa tsa thuso tsa SARS, litsebi tsa lekhetho, kapa Setsi sa Puisano (0800 00 7277) haeba o hloka thuso.

Ntlafatsa Lintlha

Boloka lintlha tsa hao tsa puisano le tsa banka li le morao-rao ho netefatsa puisano e bonolo le litefo tsa ho khutlisa.

Liphoso tse Tloaelehileng tse Lokelang ho Qojoa

  • Lintlha tse fosahetseng tsa banka: Hlahloba habeli linomoro tsa akhaonto bakeng sa litefo tsa ho khutlisa
  • Chelete e kenang e siuoang: Kenyelletsa MEHLOLI EOHLE ea chelete e kenang, esita le linomoro tse nyane
  • Ho batla ho ntsha ho sa amohelehe: Batla feela litjeho tse molao, tse tšehetsoeng
  • Ho hlokomoloa ha data e tlatsoang esale pele: Netefatsa 'me o lokise tlhahisoleseling efe kapa efe e fosahetseng e tlatsoang esale pele
  • Ho romela morao: Fana pele ho nako ea ho fela ho qoba litlolo
  • Ho se boloke lirekoto: Boloka litokomane tse tšehetsang litlhoko tsohle
