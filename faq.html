Q: What is taxable income?
Taxable income is your total income minus exempt income and allowable deductions, including salary, bonuses, allowances, and investment income.

Q: What deductions can I claim?
Common deductions include retirement fund contributions, medical aid contributions, professional development expenses, travel expenses, and home office expenses.
Q: When is the tax deadline?
Non-provisional taxpayers: 31 October 2025. Provisional taxpayers: 31 January 2026.
